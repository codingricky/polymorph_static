Privacy Policy for Pommer

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Thank you for using Pommer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Pommer collects the following types of information:

1. Information Collected for Advertising

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob, which may collect certain information to serve ads, including:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as advertising ID (IDFA) for iOS devices
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with ads and the app
  • Advertising Data: Data used to display relevant advertisements

This data is collected by Google and is used to provide personalized advertisements. For more information about how Google uses this data, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Information You Provide

Pommer stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Timer Settings: Your preferred focus and break durations
  • Statistics: Your completed focus sessions and productivity data

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used for:

  • Displaying advertisements through our advertising partner, Google AdMob
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Analyzing app performance and crash reports

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information collected by Google AdMob is subject to Google's privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policy for details on their data handling.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy

Your Choices

You can control personalized advertising on your device:

  • iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising and toggle off "Personalized Ads"
  • You may also reset your advertising identifier in Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising

Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device (settings and statistics) remains until you delete the app or clear the app's data. Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Pommer is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
